Copy the FFA320Connector folder to the plugins subfolder of your Flight Factor A320 Ultimate aircraft.

So in the end it should look something like this:

  X-Plane 11\Aircraft\Flight Factor A320 Ultimate\plugins\FFA320Connector

or

  X-Plane 10\Aircraft\Flight Factor A320 Ultimate\plugins\FFA320Connector

depending on your version of X-Plane.
